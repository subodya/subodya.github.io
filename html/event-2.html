BBQ NIGHTS

Experience the ultimate beachside BBQ party every Sunday night on the sandy shores of Tangalle Beach.

With the sound of the waves in the background and the bonfire's warm glow, our BBQ Night is a mesmerizing blend of flavors, music, and dancing. It's an enchanting evening that captures the essence of coastal nightlife.

Event Details

  • Date: Sunday Night
  • Time: 7:00 PM - 4:00 AM
  • Location: Tangalle Beach (100m From The Hotel)
